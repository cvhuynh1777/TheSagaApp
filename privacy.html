PRIVACY POLICY

// LAST UPDATED: JUNE 2026

// WHO WE ARE

SAGA is a personal quarterly planning web app. We are a solo indie product. Questions? Email us at christina.v.huynh1@gmail.com.

// WHAT WE COLLECT

We collect your email address when you sign up. We store the planning data you create in the app (quests, trackers, shop items, highlights). We do not collect your name, phone number, or any payment card data — payments are handled entirely by Stripe.

// HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Your email is used to log you in and send you transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset). If you opted in at signup, we may send occasional product updates. Your planning data is stored solely to power your app experience — we do not sell it, share it, or analyze it for advertising.

// PAYMENTS

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/privacy.

// DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS). It is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Row Level Security so only your account can access your data.

// YOUR RIGHTS

You can delete your account at any time from the account settings. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our systems. You can also email us to request a copy of your data or to have it deleted manually.

// COOKIES

We use only a session cookie required for authentication. No tracking cookies, no third-party advertising cookies.

// CHANGES

We may update this policy. If changes are significant we will notify you by email.
